What CacheLint Catches

90 checks across 6 categories, covering every aspect of caching correctness and operational safety.

CI
🔄

Cache Invalidation

Detects missing invalidation after write operations, stale data persisting after mutations, partial invalidation leaving orphaned keys, and cache-source divergence patterns throughout your codebase.

15 checks
TE
⏳

TTL & Expiry

Finds missing TTL on cache.set calls, TTLs that are excessively long or dangerously short, no jitter on expiration times, hardcoded magic numbers for durations, and inconsistent expiry policies.

15 checks
CS
🐂

Cache Stampede

Catches missing locking on cache miss paths, thundering herd risks on key expiry, absent singleflight patterns, no early recompute before expiration, and concurrent rebuild scenarios that crush your backend.

15 checks
RM
🔴

Redis/Store Misuse

Detects KEYS * in production code, missing connection pooling, unbounded lists and sets growing without limits, FLUSHALL in application code, blocking calls on the main thread, and missing error handling.

15 checks
CA
🏗

Cache Architecture

Catches cache-aside without fallback handling, N+1 cache get patterns inside loops, missing eviction policies, mixing write-through and write-behind strategies, and caching without size limits or monitoring.

15 checks
SH
🛡

Security & Hygiene

Finds sensitive data stored without encryption, PII embedded in cache keys, Redis instances without authentication, cache key injection vulnerabilities, and missing access controls on shared cache layers.

15 checks
